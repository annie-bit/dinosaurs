3 Great Places To Visit In New York

  1. Central Park

    Central Park is an amazing place. It is extemely large and resides in the center of Manhattan, New York. It consists of 5 parks.

  2. Statue Of Liberty

    The Statue Of Liberty is located on Staten Island, New York. It was a gift from the French to the USA. It was dedicated on October 28, 1886. It's metal and framework was designed by Gustave Eiffel, who also designed the Eiffel tower named after him.

  3. Empire State Building

    The Empire State Building consists of 102 stories. It is an Art Deco skyscraper in Midtown Manhattan in New York City, United States. It was designed by Shreve, Lamb & Harmon and built from 1930 to 1931. Its name is derived from "Empire State", the nickname of the state of New York.
