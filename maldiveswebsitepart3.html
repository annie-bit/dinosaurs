You're gonna love Maldives

Mirihi Island

Mirihi Island is a small luxury island that can be one of the best places to visit in Maldives. This island offers excellent water villas for your ultimate pleasure. If you’re especially looking for a place cut off from the rest of the world, this can be the perfect place.










Huvahendhoo Island

If you’re looking for that perfect destination for a family vacation, look no further than Huvahendhoo Island. The island is custom-made to accommodate families and kids. The island has a good collection of restaurants, resorts and spas. It also has a Kids Club.























Rangali Island

The Conrad Maldives resort on Rangali Island is an exceptional place for an all-round Maldives experience for families. Those looking for a place that offers fun activities for adults and kids alike can always go here. Indulge in snorkelling, fishing and dolphin sighting or simply relax in the infinity pool where perfectly blue water never ends.
